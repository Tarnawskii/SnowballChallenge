The Problem

We in BinBees aim to reduce garbage in the streets to zero. Our bee drones will detect garbage, pick it up and fly it to the nearest bin with it. We help maintaining a clean and healthy evironment in urban areas. No more streets overflowing with garbage, and no more plastic in our oceans. BinBees are the solution to garbage pollution.
