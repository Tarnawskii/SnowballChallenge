The Problem

There is a problem with trash all over the world, in parks, streets and beaches. This is something that both ruins the nature and makes it less enjoyable. This is something that we in BinBees see as a major issue that we are passionate about. We in BinBees have come up with a solution to this problem.

Solution

We in BinBees aim to reduce garbage in the streets to zero. Our bee drones will detect garbage, pick it up and fly it to the nearest bin. Our bees will help maintaining a clean and healthy environment in public areas. There will be no more streets and parks overflowing with garbage. BinBees are the solution to garbage pollution.
